How to Play

  • Tap - Reveal a cell
  • Swipe Up - Place or remove a flag
  • Swipe Down - Reveal neighbors (when flags match number)

FAQ

How do I restore my ad removal purchase?

Go to Records screen and tap "Restore Purchase" at the bottom.

How do I reset my records?

Go to Records screen and tap "Clear Records" at the bottom.
